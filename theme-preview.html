Stuck in the Mud with No Traction

You can feel a capable team working harder every week while progress quietly slips. The issue isn't talent — it's the system. This chapter is the turning point.

Step Inside Take the Assessment Jump to References

Step Inside

I've been there.

Watching a capable team work harder each week while progress quietly slips. Plans expand. Dependencies multiply. Confidence erodes — not because the team lacks talent, but because the system isn't supporting the work.

At the core of the Right-Sized Framework is a six-phase product development cycle:

  • Product–Market Fit — solving a real problem
  • Plan — aligning around achievable direction
  • Design — turning intent into solutions
  • Build — effort into capability

Action Plan

Four moves that change the trajectory.

Diagnose & learn

  1. Take the RSF Assessment. Identify where structural constraints limit progress.
  2. Read the Book. Understand the framework end-to-end.

Deploy & lead

  1. Engage your team. Direct section leads to the tools.
  2. Lead deliberately. Aligned leadership sustains discipline over time.
